## Overview

Freestyle JPEGs allow users to create their own digital assets with custom scarcity and pricing. Unlike themed photos, freestyle JPEGs are market-driven assets that can be traded, enabling influencers and artists to monetize their personal brand directly.

## Key Differences from Themed Photos

| Feature | Themed Photos | Freestyle JPEGs |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Scarcity** | Unlimited submissions | User-set (e.g., 1/100) |
| **Pricing** | Free (earn 100 PX) | User-set (PX or crypto) |
| **Time Decay** | Yes (collects decay over 24h) | No (market-driven) |
| **Purpose** | Daily contest participation | Asset creation & trading |
| **Revenue** | 20% royalties + 100 PX | 100% direct sales |

## Creating Freestyle JPEGs

### Step 1: Set Scarcity

Choose how many editions to create:
- **1/1** - Unique, one-of-a-kind
- **1/10** - Limited edition of 10
- **1/100** - Limited edition of 100
- Or any number you choose

<Info>
  Scarcity creates perceived value. Limited editions often command higher prices.
</Info>

### Step 2: Set Price

Price your JPEG in:
- **Pixels (PX)** - In-game currency
- **Cryptocurrency** - Direct crypto payment
- **$JPEG** - Governance token

### Step 3: Upload & Publish

Upload your photo and publish your freestyle JPEG. It becomes immediately available for purchase.

## Trading Dynamics

Freestyle JPEGs operate like NFT marketplaces:

- **Primary Sales:** Direct purchase from creator
- **Secondary Trading:** JPEGs can be resold
- **Market Value:** Determined by supply, demand, and creator reputation
- **Scarcity Premium:** Limited editions often appreciate
