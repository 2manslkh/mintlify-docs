## How It Works

### Sponsored Themed Weeks

Brands can sponsor themed weeks with custom prompts:

**Example: Nike's "Just Do It" Week**
- Branded theme prompts (e.g., "Your Active Moment")
- Bounty rewards for top submissions
- Authentic UGC generation
- Viral marketing potential

### Bounty System

Brands set bounties for themed content:

- **Bounty Amount:** Set by brand (e.g., $500, $1,000)
- **Theme:** Custom prompt aligned with brand
- **Participation:** 10-20% of users typically participate
- **Results:** 50-100 photos per $500 bounty

### Freestyle JPEG Collections

Brands can sponsor freestyle JPEG collections:

- Limited edition branded JPEGs
- Influencer partnerships
- Cultural alignment
- Extended engagement
