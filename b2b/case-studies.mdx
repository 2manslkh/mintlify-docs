## Starbucks: Morning Coffee Campaign

### Campaign Overview

**Brand:** Starbucks  
**Theme:** "You Before Your Morning Coffee"  
**Bounty:** $1,000  
**Duration:** 1 week

### Results

- **Submissions:** 200 photos
- **Social Impressions:** 10,000+
- **Engagement Rate:** 3x higher than traditional ads
- **Cost per Impression:** $0.10 (vs. $0.50+ for traditional)

### Analysis

The campaign leveraged JPEG's community creativity to generate authentic content that resonated with Starbucks' audience. The "You Before Your Morning Coffee" theme was relatable and encouraged playful, genuine submissions.

**Key Success Factors:**
- Engaging, relatable theme
- Appropriate bounty amount
- Active community participation
- Strong brand alignment

<Info>
  Based on Instagram's 2024 UGC campaigns averaging 50 impressions per post, the campaign generated significant reach at a fraction of traditional advertising costs.
</Info>

## Lessons Learned

### What Works

✅ **Engaging Themes:** Relatable, creative prompts drive participation  
✅ **Appropriate Bounties:** Adequate rewards ensure quality submissions  
✅ **Brand Alignment:** Themes that match brand values perform better  
✅ **Community Engagement:** Active brand participation increases enthusiasm  
✅ **Extended Duration:** Week-long campaigns generate more content  

### Challenges

⚠️ **Theme Selection:** Vague themes reduce quality  
⚠️ **Bounty Sizing:** Too low reduces participation  
⚠️ **Timing:** Campaign timing affects engagement  
⚠️ **Moderation:** Ensuring brand-appropriate content  

## Best Practices

### Campaign Planning

1. **Define Goals:** Clear objectives guide theme selection
2. **Know Your Audience:** Understand JPEG's community
3. **Set Budget:** Appropriate bounties drive participation
4. **Plan Timeline:** Optimal duration maximizes engagement

### Execution

1. **Engage Actively:** Participate in community during campaign
2. **Provide Feedback:** Acknowledge submissions
3. **Share Results:** Amplify winning content
4. **Measure Impact:** Track metrics for future campaigns

### Optimization

1. **Test Themes:** Experiment with different prompts
2. **Analyze Results:** Learn from each campaign
3. **Iterate:** Improve based on data
4. **Build Relationships:** Long-term partnerships drive success
